Paint Calculator Test Plan

Objectives
-Provide a set of automated tests that verify the accuracy of the Paint Calculator
-Document any defects and address areas that are lacking requirements

In Scope
-Automated functional tests
-Single browser and OS with the use of Selenium

Out of Scope
-Performance, Stress, Load, and Volume testing
-Multiple browsers and OSs

Approach
-Start with basic use cases such as single and multi-room scenarios
-Include negative tests to verify no bad data is entered
